I'm a third-year PhD student in Information Science (joint degree in Cognitive Science) at the University of Colorado Boulder, advised by Martha Palmer. My research interests focus on computational semantics, ontology design, and social informatics.


I completed my B.A. (2016) in Linguistics at the University of Texas at Austin and my M.Sc. (2018) in Computational Linguistics at the University at Buffalo. My complete CV can be found here.


In my free time, I enjoy painting, biking, exploring (and occasionally writing about) new avenues for culinary creativity, and skating (ice/inline).
